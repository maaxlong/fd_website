Frankie Dytor

Frankie Dytor is an academic, writer, and critic.

Frankie’s first book, Queer Practices of Art History, is forthcoming with Cambridge University Press. They have written on art and books for Review 31, The Conversation, Modernist Review, and Lucy Writers. Frankie is a co-curator of the film collective Queer Classics (@queer.classics)

They are currently a British Academy Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Exeter.

For enquiries and commissions, please contact their agent Imogen Morrell at: imorrell@greeneheaton.co.uk.
